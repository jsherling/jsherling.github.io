Miguel de Cervantes

Guest Author

Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra is a Spanish writer widely regarded as the greatest writer in the Spanish language and one of the world’s pre-eminent novelists. He has just published Don Quixote, a novel we have been told that will have a sequel. It’s getting rave reviews so far, with one critic saying it is one of the pinnacles of world literature.
